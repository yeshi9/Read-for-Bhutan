Mission


The mission of Read for Bhutan is "To maintain and improve the quality of life for all citizens of our community by providing resources and even some programmes that enhance and contribute to individual's knowledge, enlightenment and enjoyment." We are committed to foster a love of reading, improve literacy skills and support the emotional well-being in our communities. We believe in the power of educational systematic racism, poverty and social inequality. To support every individual of our nation in developing lifelong literacy skills, Read for Bhutan is committed in making books a part of every individual's life.
To accomplish our mission, we have books to download given in the product link. You can download it from there :).


About me


I am Yeshi Dema. We all might be knowing how important is reading to us. Our government is working so hard in enhancing the citizens of Bhutan starting from the young children to indulge in reading. Every schools are having reading hours apart from their subject matters. Even the Bhutan Broadcasting Services have made programmes on reading whereby adults and students used to narrate or either review on what they have read. So, I as a Bhutanese citizen would like to contribute to the community in one way or other by offering some free book downloads on our website. If you all have any book donations to be made, do email me or leave a comment down below. Every week, the downloads will be updated. So stay tuned and Happy Reading!!!
